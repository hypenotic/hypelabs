HypeLabs

The toolkits and guidelines that power Hypenotic projects.

Hypenotic is a founding Canadian B Corp on a mission to manifest a more Generative Economy. Beyond sustainable, a Generative approach looks for opportunities to spark other positive returns.

This handbook is an attempt at codifying our most recent thinking and processes. These ideas have come out of many years of learning from missteps and successes. The point of making these decisions is to ensure we're all on the same page, and that we can easily focus on what was intended for any project.

Maintained by your friendly neighbourhood humans at Hypenotic.
